Rebuy
How rebuy works

Log the date you bought on each account. Each account reopens 8 days later — that's when it shows rebuy now and appears in your to-do list. The countdown runs from the purchase date, not delivery, so you're prompted regardless of when stock lands. Always re-check the price before buying again — deals move. Marking stock arrived is optional, just a note for yourself. Not ready when it opens? Snooze 1d pushes it a day.
